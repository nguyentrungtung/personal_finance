# Project Brief: COURTIFY - Elite Personal Finance Management

## 1. Project Overview
**COURTIFY** is a high-fidelity, premium personal finance management platform designed for investors and high-net-worth individuals who require "Elite Precision" in tracking their wealth. The application focuses on sophisticated asset tracking, loan management, and financial planning within a unified, high-contrast dark-mode interface.

## 2. Core Value Proposition
- **High-Precision Tracking**: Detailed ledgers for diverse assets including Gold, Silver, Real Estate, and Savings.
- **Centralized Management**: Unified views for loans, debts, and financial events.
- **Professional Aesthetic**: A "Midnight Velocity" design language that emphasizes focus, speed, and premium status.

## 3. Target Audience
- Individual investors managing diverse portfolios.
- Professionals tracking personal lending and borrowing.
- Users seeking a high-performance, dark-themed financial dashboard.

## 4. Feature Requirements

### 4.1. Dashboard & Analytics
- **Overview**: Real-time visualization of net worth, asset distribution, and portfolio health.
- **Analytics**: Trend analysis and predictive modeling for future financial positions.

### 4.2. Asset Ledger
- **Multi-Asset Support**: Specialized entry forms for different asset classes.
- **Gold/Silver Ledger**: Track weight, purity, and purchase price.
- **Investment Tracking**: Detailed records of principal amounts, interest rates, and maturity dates.

### 4.3. Loan & Debt Management
- **Counterparty Tracking**: Manage who you've lent to or borrowed from.
- **Status Monitoring**: Visual indicators for Active, Overdue, and Settled transactions.
- **Repayment Scheduling**: Track due dates and installment plans.

### 4.4. Financial Calendar
- **Event Logging**: Schedule investment milestones, debt repayments, and savings goals.
- **Reminders**: Integrated notification system for upcoming financial obligations.

### 4.5. System Configuration
- **Centralized Institutions**: Manage a list of banks and financial firms in Settings to populate dropdowns app-wide.
- **Localization**: Multi-currency support (defaulting to VND) and timezone-aware inputs.

## 5. Design System: Midnight Velocity
- **Theme**: Ultra-dark mode (Surface: #131313).
- **Primary Color**: Sport Green (#22c55e) for actions and positive growth.
- **Typography**: Inter (Sans-serif) for high readability and professional tone.
- **Shape**: Rounded Eight (8px border-radius) for a modern, accessible feel.

## 6. Technical Constraints
- **Device**: Optimized for Desktop (high-density information display).
- **Precision**: 64-bit float precision for all currency calculations.
- **Locale**: Primary focus on Asia/Ho_Chi_Minh (GMT+07:00).
